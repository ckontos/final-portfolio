A Little About Myself

I am an educated Full Stack Web Developer with a true passion for front-end web development. I graduated from the University of North Carolina at Charlotte with a degree in Exercise Science, and also with a Full-Stack Web Development graduate certificate.

My love for health and fitness in both the past and present, has given me a deep rooted sense of disicpline and determination that drive me in everything I do. I am a problem solver to my core. I specialize in identifying an end product and finding the most efficient route to get there in a time sensitive manner. Web development has given me the ability to do that everyday, and build websites that come to life.

I am skilled in many front and back end technologies. In terms of styling, I am strong with CSS, SASS, and CSS framworks such as Materialize and Bootstrap. I am skilled with javascript, jQuery, the MERN stack, and mySQL. I am a quick study. I spend each day increasing my competancy in each of these skills, and also in new technologies as well.
