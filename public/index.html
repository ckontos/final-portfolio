Hey! Meet Dimitri.

Dimitri Kontos is a Full Stack Web Developer with a true passion for front end web development. He graduated from the University of North Carolina at Charlotte with a degree in Exercise Science, and also with a Full-Stack Web Development graduate certificate.

Dimitri specializes in creating the most functional, vibrant, and interactive front-end interfaces for his clients. Dimitri looked into Web Development to cure his need for creative freedom. He found that putting his ideas and creativity into building web sites was something that could help him reach the creative potential he felt he could express through his work.

His experiences have molded him into an individual that takes great pride in his work and the experience he gives his clients. He brings a fresh, dynamic, and creative mind to the field of web development. One line of code at a time, Dimitri intends on using his drive, discipline, and dedication to make a difference with his work.
